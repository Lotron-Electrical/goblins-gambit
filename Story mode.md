Goblins Gambit story mode

When signed in you navigate to the story mode button.
When it starts it will give you a quick run down of what this mode does and how it works.
It will then prompt you to create a card. Here the player will enter a name for their new creature.
This will be their first on only creature they can create. It will only exist within the context of the story mode gameplay.
With the name chosen it will enter them into the first map. This will be a modelled of the basic drawing in Goblins-gambit>Screenshots>Story mode progression map. It should start the player in one spot with the option to branch left or right. when they move down the line to the next stop this will start their first story mode encounter.

This first game should be really easy playing against a story mode bot. The difference is that they will have the card that they named and created. This card will have base stats of 100 ATK, 100 DEF, and SP 100. This card will come with the player through their story mode game. It will scale over time with upgrades and enhancements. This first encounter will play out quite simply. If the player dies in any of these story mode games they lose one of 3 lives. If all lives are lost the story mode will end. If they win they will be able to decide where they go based off the branch map based on the drawing in Goblins-gambit>Screenshots>Story mode progression map.

Each stop on the progression map will be a mix of battles with enemies scaling in difficulty and enhancement opportunities. these will be spread out randomly each new time a player chooses story mode to keep the gameplay fresh. just like the game peglin or inscryption.

There should be 6 maps with a chance of at least 5 battles and enhancement opportunities. The first stage should be called the tavern, the second hills, third the swamp, fourth the tundra, fifth the cliffs, sixth will be the volcano.

The tavern

- easy to fight bots in encounters
- limited and easy cards to play with and fight with.
- simple gameplay mechanics at the start.

The hills

- Slightly harder bots. not quite medium but enough to challenge new players.
- a few more cards than before.
- Enhancement options for player made card like Gain 100 ATK or DEF. gain a special ability like "sweep" split all ATK across all ops cards" or Dodge "when op attacks card it dodges but the attack hits players SP instead". With each upgrade that cards SP value should increase as well.

The swamp

- This should be all the cards playable
- medium difficulty
- more upgrades for the player made cards.
- other enhancements like gain one life back, Increase the chance of their card to go back into the pick up pile for them to draw.

The tundra

- This should be set to the frost game setting of the regular game as well as the bots getting a little harder than medium
- More enhancements for players cards
- more enhancements like a one time use of a bezerk charm that allows the player to use at any time and doubles all ATK of creatures for 5 rounds of gameplay.

The cliffs

- This should be set to the blood moon settings of the regular game well as the bots getting a little harder than medium
- More enhancements for players cards. This is where if played right the players player made card can really become super strong
- more enhancements like a one time use of a sock satchel portal which will allow them to use a card they have already made in a previous story mode play through. If this is their first time then this option wont present itself.

The volcano

- This will be an all out battle with the bots set to hard
- No upgrades for the players player made card
- lots of one single use items. ( will be defined in the future)

I want the base gameplay loop to be the player going through each encounter either battles or enhancement opportunities (about 5 of a mix of both per level). Then reaching the next level. The players understanding of the game should be getting better as well as their access to better cards and upgrades. The player made cards is a new mechanic only for story mode. The cards the player makes should be stored in a trophy cambinat that can be viewed at any time at the end of the story mode run or the story mode menu. They also will be stored so that they can be called into play using the sock satchel portal if a player gains that ability in the cliffs level.
