1. Overview

Story Mode is a single-player roguelike campaign. You create a custom creature card and battle through 6 themed levels of increasing difficulty. Between battles, you upgrade your card via enhancements. The run ends when you defeat all 6 bosses or lose all your lives.

Each run features a randomized branching map mixing battles and enhancement opportunities — no two runs are the same. Inspired by games like Peglin and Inscryption.

2. Starting a Run

Card Creation

At the start of each run, you create your custom creature card:

  • Enter a creature name (max 20 characters)
  • Choose from 4 card art options
  • Optionally enable Nightmare Mode

This card is unique to your run. It starts with base stats and evolves through enhancements as you progress.

Starting Stats

ModeATKDEFSPLives
Normal 100 100 100 3
Nightmare 50 50 50 2
Your custom card costs 1 AP to play, has 1 copy in your deck, and starts with no special ability. You can gain an ability through enhancements later.

4. The Dungeon Map

Each level has a branching node map rendered as a pixel-art dungeon crawler.

Map Structure

  • 5 rows of nodes (rows 0–4)
  • Row 0: Single start node — always a battle
  • Rows 1–3: 2–3 nodes each — mix of battles (~60%) and enhancements (~40%)
  • Row 4: Single boss node
  • Each node connects to 1–2 nodes in the next row
  • You can only move forward — no backtracking

Navigation

  • Click/tap to move your character through the dungeon
  • Rooms are connected by corridors with doors
  • Locked doors indicate nodes you can't reach yet
  • Fog of war hides unexplored areas (4-tile visibility radius)
  • Walking to a room center triggers the encounter
  • After defeating a boss, stairs appear leading to the next level

5. Battles

Story mode battles follow the same rules as regular Goblins Gambit — you play cards, summon creatures, cast spells, and race to the SP win threshold. The key difference is you always fight a bot opponent that scales in difficulty with each level.

Battle Setup

  • Win SP threshold randomly chosen from the level's range
  • Your custom card is always in your starting hand
  • Level theme effects are active (see Theme Effects)
  • AP = Base (2) + level bonus + encumbrance modifiers - theme penalties

Available Abilities

Sweep — Split ATK across all opponent creatures
Dodge — Dodge attacks (damage hits SP instead)
Streamer Draw — Draw 1 card when played

Burn — Bonus SP on kill

No stat caps! Your card's stats can grow indefinitely. A well-enhanced card can reach 500+ in all stats by the end of a run.

7. Items

Items are one-time-use consumables activated during any battle.

Berserk Charm

Effect: Doubles the ATK of all your creatures for 10 turns.

Available from: Tundra, Cliffs, Volcano

Sock Satchel Portal

Effect: Opens your trophy cabinet — choose a card from a previous run to summon into your hand.

Available from: Cliffs, Volcano

Requires trophy cards from completed runs.

10. Victory & Defeat

Victory

Defeat all 6 level bosses to complete the run.

Your custom card is saved as a trophy.

Options: View Trophies, Play Again, Back to Lobby

Defeat

Lose all your lives at any point.

Your card is still saved as a trophy (showing level reached).

Options: Play Again, Back to Lobby

Run Over Stats

The end screen displays your full run statistics:

  • Battles won / lost
  • Levels cleared
  • Enhancements picked
  • Total SP earned
  • Creatures killed
  • Final custom card stats

11. Trophies & Achievements

After every run (win or lose), your custom card is saved permanently as a trophy. Trophies can be viewed from the story mode menu and can be summoned in future runs via the Sock Satchel Portal item.

13. Theme Effects

Each level applies a battlefield theme that modifies core game mechanics.

ThemeATKDEFSpell CostAP PenaltyBerserk
Swamp (Tavern, Hills, Swamp) 1x1x1xNoneOff
Frost (Tundra) 1x1.5xFree!-1 AP/turnOff
Blood Moon (Cliffs) 1.5x1x2x APNone2x dmg
Inferno (Volcano) 1.5x1.5xFree!None2x dmg
Swamp — Standard rules, no modifiers. The baseline experience.

Frost — Defensive meta. DEF is boosted 50%, spells are free, but you get 1 less AP per turn.

Blood Moon — Aggressive meta. ATK boosted 50%, berserk doubles damage for last place, but spells cost double AP.

Inferno — Everything cranked up. Both ATK and DEF boosted 50%, spells are free, and berserk is always active. The ultimate challenge.
